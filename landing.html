EcoNomix — Finance Helper Agent

Whether for a startup founder, a student fest organizer, or a small business owner.

Managing money is tough — whether for a startup founder, a student fest organizer, or a small business owner. People ask:

  • Startup: "If I hire 2 more engineers, can I survive 6 months?"
  • Student Fest: "If we spend ₹10,000 extra on marketing, what's left for prizes?"
  • Small Business: "If I increase product price by 10%, what happens to profit?"

Most use Excel, but those don't show clear 'what-if' outcomes. EcoNomix lets you adjust inputs, see effects on budget/runway with charts, and generate shareable reports for any financial scenario.

Core Features

Everything You Need for Budget Scenarios

Interactive tools designed for clear 'what-if' financial analysis

Multi-Domain Scenario Builder

Flexible inputs for any domain: hiring costs, marketing spend, price changes, event expenses, or operational costs. Perfect for startups, events, and businesses.

Smart Financial Forecasting

Get instant insights: runway calculations for startups, budget allocation for events, profit projections for businesses. All with interactive charts and clear summaries.

Professional Reports & Sharing

Generate investor-ready reports for startups, event budget summaries for organizers, or business financial plans. Share via PDF, JSON, or secure links.

Flexible Pricing Model

Pay only for what you use: ₹10 per scenario, ₹50 per report. Perfect for budget-conscious startups, student organizations, and small businesses.

Live Data Integration

Connect to real financial data sources. Whether it's bank accounts, accounting software, or event management tools - get fresh data for accurate projections.

Usage Tracking & Insights

Track your financial planning activity: scenarios tested, reports generated, and costs. Get insights to optimize your budgeting workflow across all domains.
